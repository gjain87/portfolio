Prediction of Helmet in Bike Riders

Project brief:

This project aims to improve road safety and encourage helmet adoption among bike riders through AI-powered detection and awareness-raising measures. The core functionality comprises:
1. Helmet Detection: Leveraging computer vision technology, the project will employ a real-time helmet detection system at strategic locations (e.g., traffic junctions). The system will analyze incoming video footage to identify bike riders and automatically determine if they are wearing helmets.
2. Violation Capture: In cases where a helmet is not detected, the system will trigger the capture of both the rider's image and the bike's license plate number. This captured information will be securely stored and used for further action, such as generating warning messages or triggering communication with traffic authorities.
